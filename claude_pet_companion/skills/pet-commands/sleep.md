# Sleep Pet Command

Put your pet to sleep or wake it up.

## Usage

```
/pet:sleep
```

## Effects

**When putting to sleep:**
- Pet enters sleep mode
- Energy recovers over time
- Immune to stat decay while sleeping

**When waking up:**
- Pet wakes up with restored energy
- Small energy cost for waking

## Auto-Sleep

Your pet will automatically suggest sleep between 10 PM - 6 AM.
