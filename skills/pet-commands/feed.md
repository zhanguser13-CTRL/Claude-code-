# Feed Pet Command

Feed your pet companion to restore hunger.

## Usage

```
/pet:feed [amount]
```

## Arguments

- `amount` (optional): Amount to feed, defaults to 30

## Effects

- Restores hunger stat
- Small happiness boost
- Special bonus during meal times!

## Meal Time Bonuses

| Meal | Hours | Bonus |
|------|-------|-------|
| Breakfast | 7-9 | +15 hunger, +20 XP |
| Lunch | 12-14 | +15 hunger, +20 XP |
| Dinner | 18-20 | +15 hunger, +20 XP |
