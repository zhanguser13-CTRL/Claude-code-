# Play Pet Command

Play with your pet companion to increase happiness.

## Usage

```
/pet:play
```

## Effects

- Significantly increases happiness
- Uses some energy (playing is tiring!)
- Small hunger cost
- Grants +15 XP

## Notes

- Your pet needs energy to play
- Playing when energy is low isn't effective
- Great for cheering up a sad pet!
